# Expanded Classification System & Data Structure Guide

## Additional Classification Categories

### 1. Meeting Requirements
**Purpose**: Determines what type of client interaction is needed

**Options:**
- **Quick Call (15-30 min)** - Brief phone discussion, simple explanation
- **Standard Meeting (45-60 min)** - Office or video meeting, moderate complexity
- **Comprehensive Review (90-120 min)** - In-depth analysis and planning session
- **Multi-Meeting Strategy** - Requires multiple touchpoints over time
- **Written Communication Only** - Email/letter sufficient, no meeting needed
- **Annual Review Agenda Item** - Add to next scheduled review

**Use Case**: Helps with calendar planning and sets client expectations

---

### 2. Professional Coordination Required
**Purpose**: Identifies if external expertise needed

**Options:**
- **Self-Sufficient** - Financial advisor handles independently
- **CPA Coordination** - Tax professional input needed
- **Estate Attorney** - Legal documents or trust work required
- **Insurance Specialist** - Advanced underwriting or product expertise
- **Broker-Dealer Compliance** - Requires internal compliance review
- **Multi-Disciplinary** - Multiple specialists needed

**Use Case**: Ensures proper resources engaged before client outreach

---

### 3. Data Dependency Level
**Purpose**: Indicates how much data analysis is required before presentation

**Options:**
- **Minimal (Basic CRM Data)** - Name, age, account types sufficient
- **Standard (Product Details)** - Requires product specs, values, dates
- **Advanced (Portfolio Analysis)** - Needs position-level, performance data
- **Comprehensive (Multi-Source)** - Requires data from multiple systems/sources
- **External Data Required** - Needs market data, benchmarks, or third-party info

**Use Case**: Helps estimate prep time and data gathering requirements

---

### 4. Client Communication Strategy
**Purpose**: Determines how to approach the client

**Options:**
- **Proactive Outreach - High Priority** - Reach out immediately, urgent matter
- **Proactive Outreach - Standard** - Schedule conversation, important but not urgent
- **Next Scheduled Touch** - Add to agenda for next planned interaction
- **Educational First** - Send article/info, then follow up
- **Opt-In Only** - Only discuss if client raises topic
- **Batch with Others** - Combine with other opportunities in one conversation

**Use Case**: Manages client communication cadence and prevents over-communication

---

### 5. Implementation Complexity
**Purpose**: Estimates effort to execute once client agrees

**Options:**
- **Simple (1-2 steps)** - Quick execution, minimal paperwork
- **Moderate (3-5 steps)** - Standard process, normal documentation
- **Complex (6-10 steps)** - Multi-step process, significant paperwork
- **Extensive (10+ steps)** - Major undertaking, project management needed
- **Ongoing Monitoring** - Requires periodic review and adjustment

**Use Case**: Sets realistic expectations and helps with workload planning

---

### 6. Competitive Sensitivity
**Purpose**: Indicates risk of competitor identifying same opportunity

**Options:**
- **Low (Proactive Only)** - Unlikely competitors would identify without prompting
- **Medium (Industry Standard)** - Common industry practice, some competitor awareness
- **High (Market Driven)** - Competitors likely marketing similar opportunities
- **Critical (Time Sensitive)** - High risk of losing to competitor if delayed

**Use Case**: Helps prioritize outreach to prevent client poaching

---

### 7. Seasonality & Timing Window
**Purpose**: Defines when scenario is relevant (beyond just urgency)

**Options:**
- **Year-Round** - Always applicable
- **Q1 Focus (Jan-Mar)** - Beginning of year opportunities
- **Q2 Focus (Apr-Jun)** - Mid-year reviews
- **Q3 Focus (Jul-Sep)** - Late summer planning
- **Q4 Focus (Oct-Dec)** - Year-end strategies
- **Tax Season (Jan-Apr 15)** - Tax filing deadline driven
- **RMD Season (Oct-Dec)** - Required distribution planning
- **Market Event Driven** - Triggered by specific market conditions
- **Regulatory Deadline** - Specific compliance or law change deadline

**Use Case**: Enables automated seasonal activation/deactivation of scenarios

---

### 8. Revenue Timing
**Purpose**: When revenue is realized (different from Revenue Potential)

**Options:**
- **Immediate (1st conversation)** - Revenue realized quickly
- **Short-term (1-3 months)** - Normal sales cycle
- **Medium-term (3-6 months)** - Longer decision process
- **Long-term (6-12 months)** - Extended planning/implementation
- **Recurring** - Ongoing fee or trail commission
- **Delayed (12+ months)** - Very long sales cycle

**Use Case**: Helps with revenue forecasting and pipeline management

---

### 9. Client Education Level Required
**Purpose**: How much education/explanation client needs

**Options:**
- **Minimal (Client Aware)** - Client likely familiar with concept
- **Basic (Quick Overview)** - Simple explanation sufficient  
- **Moderate (Detailed Explanation)** - Requires thorough education
- **Significant (Multi-Touch Education)** - Complex concept, multiple conversations
- **Expert (Third-Party Validation)** - May need external expert to explain

**Use Case**: Determines prep materials needed and conversation structure

---

### 10. Risk Profile Match
**Purpose**: Which client risk profiles this scenario suits

**Options:**
- **Conservative** - Capital preservation focused
- **Moderately Conservative** - Income with some growth
- **Moderate** - Balanced approach
- **Moderately Aggressive** - Growth with some income
- **Aggressive** - Maximum growth potential
- **All Risk Profiles** - Universally applicable
- **Risk Profile Specific** - Only for certain profiles (specify in notes)

**Use Case**: Filters scenarios by client risk tolerance during matching

---

### 11. Life Stage Relevance
**Purpose**: Which life stages this scenario typically applies to

**Options:**
- **Accumulation (Under 50)** - Building wealth
- **Peak Earning (50-60)** - Maximum income years
- **Pre-Retirement (55-65)** - Transition planning
- **Early Retirement (65-75)** - Initial retirement phase
- **Established Retirement (75+)** - Later retirement years
- **Multi-Generational** - Involves multiple generations
- **All Life Stages** - Age-agnostic opportunity

**Use Case**: Quick filter by client age/stage

---

### 12. Relationship Depth Required
**Purpose**: How established relationship needs to be

**Options:**
- **New Client Suitable** - Appropriate for prospects/new clients
- **Established Client (1+ years)** - Requires some relationship history
- **Long-Standing Client (3+ years)** - Needs deep trust and understanding
- **Inner Circle Only** - Only for closest, most sophisticated clients
- **Universal** - Suitable for any client relationship stage

**Use Case**: Prevents awkward conversations with new/less established clients

---

### 13. Documentation Requirements
**Purpose**: What paperwork/documentation is needed

**Options:**
- **Minimal (Verbal Agreement)** - Conversation notes only
- **Standard (Basic Forms)** - Normal applications/paperwork
- **Comprehensive (Extensive Documentation)** - Significant paperwork
- **Legal Documentation** - Contracts, trusts, legal forms
- **Compliance Heavy** - Requires detailed compliance documentation
- **Third-Party Forms** - Outside vendor/carrier paperwork

**Use Case**: Sets expectations for client and admin workload

---

### 14. Technology/Platform Dependency
**Purpose**: What systems are required for execution

**Options:**
- **Platform Agnostic** - No specific system required
- **Custodian Specific** - Requires specific custodian (Schwab, Fidelity, etc.)
- **Software Required** - Needs specific planning/analysis software
- **Carrier Specific** - Limited to certain insurance carriers
- **Multiple Platforms** - Requires coordination across systems
- **Manual Process** - No system support, manual execution

**Use Case**: Identifies operational limitations or requirements

---

### 15. Compliance/Suitability Complexity
**Purpose**: Level of compliance review needed

**Options:**
- **Standard Suitability** - Normal compliance review
- **Enhanced Review** - Additional compliance scrutiny
- **Complex Products** - Requires specialized compliance approval
- **Senior Investor** - Enhanced protections for age 65+
- **Accredited Investor Verification** - Must verify AI status
- **State-Specific Rules** - Varies by state, detailed review needed

**Use Case**: Flags scenarios requiring extra compliance attention
